    The PPPP Conspiracy Magazine

    The Magazine

    The PPPP Conspiracy Magazine is a weekly magazine focusing on different conspiracies from around the world. The name of the magazine derives from a famous fringe theory called "The Presentationally Perfect Powerful Publications Conspiracy". It is said that many people from around the globe are following a master's degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the University of Bologna, working on, among other projects, a Website aimed at producing Presentationally Perfect Powerful Publications.

    This magazine allows the reader to read and compare different articles dealing with conspiracies. In particular, comparisons between the occurrence of different entities in the texts are allowed, as well as the addition of personalised keywords.
